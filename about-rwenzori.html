Our Story

Founded by local guides who grew up in the shadow of the Mountains of the Moon, Rwenzori Mountain Tours was born from a simple belief: that every Ugandan deserves to know the wonders of their own country. We started with a few friends and a passion for trekking, and today we've helped hundreds discover the magic of the Rwenzori.

Our mission is to create unforgettable, authentic experiences that connect travelers with nature and culture, while supporting local communities and preserving the environment.
